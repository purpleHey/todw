Unintend'o

It's 1987, and a low budget startup video game company, Unintend'o, is trying to get into the gaming business by making knock-off games of popular titles. Their first game, entitled Peerless Fabio Twins, involves two pipefitting, Italian twin bothers, named Fabio and Lucio, who face a variety of dangers in order to rescue the Countess Lichen. To do so, they must defeat all sorts of animated lichens and molds by jumping on top of them.

You've been hired to create a new controller for the video game company Unintend'o. You will need to decide the role of each button on your controller so that you can move your character through a 2D digital space.
