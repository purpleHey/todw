Maze Game

In 1980, the video game Pac-Man was released in the United States with massive appeal. Originally, it was a single player game in which a player controls Pac-man around a maze, trying to collect all the pellets it can while avoiding attack by enemy ghosts.

Your job is to program a Pac-man-like Maze Game that includes opponents utilizing a variety of intelligent and unintelligent behaviors. You will not have to program it from scratch, though you will have to program it with Scratch! A template for the Maze Game has already been programmed for you. You will use this template, reverse engineer the artificial intelligence, and re-program it to include a variety of intelligent and unintelligent behaviors to make your game the appropriate amount of challenging, so that it's fun!
